Rocket Hat (Work in progress)

Engine:


GameMaker Studio 2.3
Originally started in GameMaker 8.1


Status: in development, no new public updates.

What I have do so far:


Reworked the majority of the game in the new engine
Updated many of the game's graphics
Improved controls and game feel

Rocket Hat is a 2D platformer I've been working on for around a year and my main project, inspired by platformers like Celeste, Rayman Legends, and the Mario & Luigi series. This game works around a very unique movement mechanic and its mid-air jump managemet. The final game will feature:

  • 5 worlds
  • various unlockable costumes and one you can edit yourself!
  • tons of replayability with two collectables, achievements, and a speedrun mode

Here are some screenshots of the first area, the Abandoned dungeon, an old and forgotten place Rocket Boy has fallen into, you'll have to find a way to the surface while finding out what has happened to his world.

Video updates can be found on my Youtube channel

The game is still to early in development so everything shown is subject to change, a site dedicated to it will be made as development goes foward. You can join my Discord server for updates.
